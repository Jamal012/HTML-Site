Football Terms

NFL
The National Football League
Interception
An interception is when an opponent on defense catches the throw not intended for him.
Fumble
The ball comes loose after the players loses control of it.
Touchdown
The player is inside the endzone with both feet inbounds with the football.
Team
A team is a member of a league with a specific number of players playing together.

There are many more terms that can be defined for people who are new to football.
